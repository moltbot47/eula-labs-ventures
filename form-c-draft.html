EULA Labs (Empowering Universal Learning through AI) is building an AI research, education, and compute center in Houston, TX — a physical campus where open-source robotics, community-governed AI agents, and developer training operate under one roof.

The company operates on a dual-track economic model:

What “Reviewed Financials” Means for a New Startup

For a newly formed LLC with no operating history, the CPA review consists of:

  • Balance sheet showing $0 or initial seed capital
  • Statement that no operations have occurred
  • CPA’s review opinion letter (limited assurance)
  • Notes to financial statements (formation date, entity purpose)

This is materially simpler and cheaper than a full audit. A competent CPA can complete this in under a week.

Confirm first-time issuer status under Rule 227.201(t)(3). Engage CPA firm to produce reviewed financial statements. For subsequent annual reports (Form C-AR), audited financials will be required if offering exceeds $1.235M threshold again.

5-Tier Contribution Scoring System

1Infrastructure Investment5xCapital investment, GPU purchases, property funding
2Compute Donation3xDonated GPU/CPU cycles, storage, bandwidth
3Agents & Code2xAI agents, software tools, integrations
4Research & Knowledge1.5xPapers, tutorials, documentation
5Community & Governance1xModeration, events, mentorship

Tokens = LLC Membership Interests

Contribution scores are tracked as non-transferable LLC membership interests that represent both governance weight and revenue share. These are structured as legal membership units under Delaware LLC law — not cryptocurrency tokens. They are earned through verified contribution, not purchased on a secondary market.

Anti-Capture Safeguards

  • 5% weight ceiling: No single member can exceed 5% of total governance weight
  • Quadratic scaling: Influence grows sub-linearly to amplify smaller contributors
  • Leadership rotation: Mandatory rotation every 6–12 months
  • Fork rights: If governance is captured, all open-source assets can be forked by the community
  • Contribution decay: Scores decay over time (Tier 1 at half speed) to maintain active participation
Review governance provisions for compliance with Delaware LLC Act. Confirm that the 5-tier scoring system and non-transferability provisions are properly documented in the Operating Agreement. Ensure that the “token” language is clearly distinguished from cryptocurrency in all SEC filings.
